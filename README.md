## Diagitizer32
Diags booting utility for many iDevices
## Supported devices
* iPad Air 1
* iPad 4
* iPad Mini 2
* iPad Mini 3
## Download
Download Diagitizer32 [here](https://github.com/Mini-Exploit/Diagitizer32/releases)
## Getting started
1. Connect a device in DFU mode.
2. Press the button.
3. If successful, your device is now in Diags Mode.
